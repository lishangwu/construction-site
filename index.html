Welcome to our site

The Metal Shop is a locally owned and operated business which supplies ACT and the surrounding region. With a wide range of Metal and Steel products, The Metal Shop caters for Engineers, Architects, Builders, Fabricators, Developers, Farmers, Trades and the DIY Specialist. We offer a prompt and reliable delivery service and no request is too big or small. We are continually growing our product line to satisfy the growing demands of our valued customers and clients. With over 40 years experience working in the trade and retail industry, The Metal Shop makes everything that is Metal, easy!
