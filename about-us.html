About Us

The Metal Shop is a Canberra based business that has evolved from over 40 years of collective experience in the retail and trade industries. We aim to provide all of our clients with the best service and product for everything metal. We stock and supply rural, commercial, architectural, semi-structural, down grade and fencing products and have the knowledge to fulfill any order. We also have the facilities to scrap soft metals and offer a prompt and reliable delivery service in the ACT region. If your business is about metal, if your business relies on metal or you are a handy man, The Metal Shop will cater for all of your needs.
